Frequently Asked Questions

It's quite simple, you are responsible for leading the workshop that you signed up for on the HobbyHacks website at the time we assigned you. Workshops are integral to helping hackers learn the skills they need to be successful during and after the event.
You do not need to purchase any resources to be a workshop lead at our event. We will provide you with all the resources you need to be successful in this role. Attendees will be notified prior to the workshop if they need to prepare or download anything.
We will send out a zoom link and workshop details to the attendees 30-minutes prior to each workshop. We ask that workshop leads arrive 10-15 minutes prior to their assigned workshop time. A TechTogether volunteer will be at every workshop to help you setup and moderate the workshop as needed. You will be given the option to record to your workshop, if you'd like the information to remain a resource for event attendees.
If your workshop/presentation is pre-recorded email the file to Fiona Whittington at fiona@techtogether.io by Monday, July 20th at the latest. We will make any file format work for us. The video will be posted in the Discord prior to the event for attendees to access before and during the event.
Here is a link to join. Feel free to join and introduce yourself at any point during the event! Our hackers would love to meet you.
You should arrive 10-15 minutes before your workshop begins to get setup and acquainted with your TechTogether volunteer.
You do not need to register for the event, but we would love to have you share the registration link with your network.
We ask that each workshop be no more than 30 minutes long, unless you've specified otherwise in your application or conversations with Fiona. If your workshop is going to be longer than 30 minutes and you haven't already let me know, please let me know as soon as possible.
The event schedule is provided below. The schedule may change as the event approaches. Please make sure to check the schedule again before the event for any updates.
